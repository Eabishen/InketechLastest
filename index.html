Junior Writer Program

The Junior Writer Program (JWP) is designed for 6 to 8 year olds to introduce them to characterization and the idea of plotting.

Young Author Program

The Young Author Program is a Creative Writing Workshop in English designed to teach children in the age group of 9 to 16 years the nuances of writing fiction.

Essay Writing Workshop

The Essay Writing Workshop is a non-fiction Writing Workshop in English designed to teach children in the age group of 9 to 16 years how to write a structured essay.
