About Us
INKEDTECH is an education company that caters to young writers. Right now, we offer three programs for children.
  • Young Author Program for ages 9 to 16
  • Junior Writer Program for ages 6 to 8
  • Essay Writing Workshop for ages 9 to 16
    • Over 200 students have participated in our writing workshops, which are conducted both online and offline for students from India and abroad. Our primary focus is to teach children in the age bracket of 6 to 16 how to write fiction and non-fiction. We provide prompts and an interactive environment, enabling the child to think freely and write with a no holds bar approach. Once the child creates the first draft, we introduce the young writer to the idea of editing and rewriting. We have helped our young participants write stories and have published more than 100 stories in eight anthologies.
    Founder and Mentor
    Neelima Vinod
    Neelima has over a decade of experience in all things writing. She has written two books - a paranormal romance novella called Unsettled where she bridges the gap between fiction and poetry and A Complete Guide to Job Placement, a book for students who are moving out of the campus to the corporate world. She holds an M. Phil degree and is alumna at the University of East Anglia (UEA) Creative Writing Workshop.
    Our Approach
    Why write fiction and non-fiction with InkedTech?
    We believe that writing is fun and can be nurtured. Like any other artistic process, writing is learned by doing, and during the span of our sessions, we provide an overview of the writing process, also providing space and time to apply what they have learned. We provide:
  • Prompts
  • Motivation (possible publication in case of fiction writing programs)
  • Interactive environment
  • Opportunity to learn by example
  • Plotting tips and a structured approach to writing stories and essays
  • Introduction to critique
    • Potential should be developed and encouraged. That’s what we do at Inkedtech. We provide the spark needed for the long journey of writing ahead!
